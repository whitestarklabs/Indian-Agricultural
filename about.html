About AgriData Explorer

India’s agricultural sector is vital, but managing agricultural data is complex and fragmented. Farmers, policymakers, and researchers face challenges accessing and analyzing data.

Project Goal

To create a data visualization platform integrating agricultural data from Indian states and districts, helping identify trends, gaps, and disparities.

Stakeholders

  • 👨‍🌾 Farmers: Informed crop choices.
  • 🏛️ Policymakers: Targeted subsidies & crop insurance.
  • 🔬 Researchers: Study climate, soil, irrigation impacts.
